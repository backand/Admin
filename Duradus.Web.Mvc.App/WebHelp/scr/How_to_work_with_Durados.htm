How to work with Durados?

  • Durados Innovative Technology
    • Complementary to today's common market business software solutions that are heavily dependent on excessive database structure, Durados’ innovative software technology platform is delivered without any prior database.
    • Durados works on any given database, providing flexibility in design and encompassing an organization’s database records, data transactions, logical processes, accessibility, monitoring, history log, advanced user settings, and more.

    • Durados business applications are offered as on-premise/internal SaaS, hybrid SaaS, or as fully cloud/external SaaS solutions that considerably reduce organizations IT hardware and software overall costs.

    • Two separate interfaces are provided by Durados – one for users (Durados Commands) and one for administrators (Admin Commands ).

      • The users interface is built according to application settings and the role of the user.
      • The administration interface acts as the center for all setting and customization operation
